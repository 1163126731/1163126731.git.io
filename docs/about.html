Peter Chan

About me

Peter was born 22 years ago in a small town called ZhongShan in China. He spent most of his years growing up in ZhongShan. At an early age, he already knew he wanted to explore the world and live a curious life. Throughout the years he developed skills that would help him through life, such as being open-minded, obtaining a positive attitude, and always learning. During high school, he wanted to start a career in business; thus he had an emphasis on learning economics and math. Little did he know, he changed his passion very fast. Peter did not enter college pursuing a business degree. He wanted to learn something different, and he chose Physics. The reason was purely because of the movie Interstellar. The mathematical logic and romance of the universe was what attracted Peter into fancying this particular topic.

Academic Career

High School 2016-2019

Peter pursued education at China Hong Kong English School in Wuguishan, ZhongShan, China. He spent three years pursuing the A-Level system, gathering analytical skills and earning an A in Economics, B in Business, and B in Math.

University of Utah 2019-2023

Graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Physics with an emphasis on computation. His favorite class was Classical Quantum Mechanics, exploring how things work like waves on a micro-scale. The skills he gained include calculus, linear algebra, and advanced computational logic.
